Welcome to Godsmark's Framing. I am Alex, producer of framed shirt and sports memoribillia and owner of the business. I am dedicated to providing the highest quality service possible, focusing on customer satisfaction, competitive pricing and flawless goods that tick all the boxes.

Humble Beginings.

The framing came by chance. My brother Nyle wanted his Scotland Commonwealth Games top framed for his birthday, so I offered to sort that out for him. After looking around different places, I realised that framing services could cost a considerable amount of money. So, I decided to research suppliers in an effort to potentially frame it myself. After some trial and error, I managed to produce something that he was impressed with. So much so that he posted a photo on his social media. This is when enquiries started rolling in from others on whether I could do some framing for them as they liked the look of the finished article. So I took this opportunity to delve down a new career path and create this business.

Why pursue it?

Having the chance to speak to people from different sporting communities is something I thoroughly enjoy. Plus, from creating the logo to setting up all the social media pages and managing their content, it excites me thinking about how my business can grow. One of my main goals is to make it easy for players and supporters from all sporting communities to get in-touch with any framing enquiries.
